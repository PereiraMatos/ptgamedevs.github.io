Our Mission

For a long time we've been missing a place where Portuguese developers could easily share doubts, achievements, new products, partnerships, ideas, dreams...

The truth is that we have a growing community. More courses, more students, more studios and, of course, more projects. It was with this growing that we decided to create this site/blog to support, inform and help grow our games industry as all those who want to enter this world.

Hope we can help to grow more and more this creative industry that allows many to dream.

Thank you!
